About Yogloans

Yogakshemam Loans Limited (formerly Yogakshemam Kuries & Loans Private Limited) had its modest beginning in the unassuming environs of Anthikad, a small village in the Thrissur district of Kerala State in the year 1991. The company was registered as a Non Banking Financial Company with the Reserve Bank of India in the year 2000 – 01. The Company having shifted its base to the heart of Thrissur Town currently operates in the 4 Southern States of Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh has 169 branches.
